Apple Inc.

NASDAQ: AAPL · USA

Last Updated: 12 Feb 2026 · By Arham Khan

Business Overview

Apple is a global technology company with a strong ecosystem of hardware, software, and services. Its recurring revenue from services adds stability.

Key Positives

  • Strong brand loyalty
  • High-margin services segment
  • Large cash reserves

Risks

  • High valuation
  • Dependence on iPhone sales
  • Regulatory pressure

QMI View

Apple remains a strong long-term company. Entry timing and valuation discipline are important for new investors.
